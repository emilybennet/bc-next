# BronyCon Announces Special Community Agents for Final Mission

(BALTIMORE) - BronyCon will always be, at its heart, an event for the community that makes it special. Over there years, there have been countless people in the “My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic” fandom that have created, inspired, and elevated projects and fans in their own special way. For the final event this August, BronyCon is happy to honor those who have made an impact on this community and welcome our lineup of Community Guests!

For it’s final mission, BronyCon has enlisted the help of 20 unique individuals from various backgrounds and professions in order to entertain and give our great event a memorable sendoff. These include a litany of artists, video creators, writers, and other personalities whose work within the community has gone up and beyond.

These people are:

## About BronyCon

Based in Baltimore, MD, the 11th and final iteration of BronyCon is scheduled for August 1-4, 2019 at the Baltimore Convention Center. The convention features special guests, diverse programming, events, and a multi-night music festival featuring some of the greatest fandom musicians. For more information, check out [bronycon.org](https://bronycon.org).
