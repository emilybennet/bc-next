Magpiepony is a brony YouTuber making pony content since the end of 2013. She's drawn millions of views for her two long-form projects _Pinkie Tales_ and _Princess Trixie Sparkle_ of which she is the writer and director. She is a notable fandom VA, singer, director, and songwriter. Her pony content as a whole has gotten more than 600 million views from her main channel and side channel _Pitch and Magpie Productions_ combined.
