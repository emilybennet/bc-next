Minty Root (Alexandre Bellefeuille) is a fan animator from Montreal, Canada known for his work on animated projects such as _Good Morning Baltimare_, _Luna's Determination_ and _The Fall of Sunset Shimmer_. At first a simple brony who tried out fan animations as a hobby, he is now using his experience as a visual effects artist in the movie industry to add a cinematic touch to his _My Little Pony_ fan animations.
