Sara Richard is an Eisner Award nominated artist. The nominated book, _Kitty & Dino_ (Yen Press) was her first children's book with more to come in the future. Sara is a frequent cover artist for IDW's _My Little Pony_ with her artwork also appearing in _Ghostbusters_ (IDW) and a variant cover for _Deadpool_ (Marvel). Sara is also the artist on the new DC _Justice League_ Tarot Card set (DC Comics) You can also find her work on issues of _Bob's Burgers_ (Dynamite) and _Rick and Morty_ (ONI Press). Her most recent book is a creator-owned original graphic novel called, _The Ghost, The Owl_ (Action Lab)."
